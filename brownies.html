Alison Brownies

2 cups flour

¾ tsp salt

6 oz baking chocolate

3 cups sugar

1 ½ cups chocolate chips

6 eggs

1 ½ tsp baking powder

1 cup butter

3 tsp vanilla

Mix flour, baking powder and salt. Melt butter and chocolate in the microwave. Beat eggs in the mixer, gradually add sugar, beat well. Add in chocolate mixture and vanilla. Mix in flour, then nuts/chocolate chips.

Spread in pre-greased pan and bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes, they should still be very runny in the middle.
