College Radio

I was the General Manager, an OTA Engineer, and a DJ at WMBR 88.1 FM, MIT's college radio station. You could listen to my show, Anything with a Beat, every other Friday at 8pm by streaming online at wmbr.org or first on your FM dial. You can also see past playlists and play back recent shows on track-blaster.com.
